Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the ground beef with garlic and onion in olive oil.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix the ricotta cheese, eggs, and half of the Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  5. Layer the lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients.
  7. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese and bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
